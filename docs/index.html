About SnapStyle AI

SnapStyle AI helps you find affordable alternatives to any clothing or shoes you love. Simply take a photo of any fashion item and our AI instantly analyzes the style, color, material, and design to find similar items at better prices.

Features

  • Visual Search - Point your camera at any fashion item
  • AI-Powered Matching - Find similar styles instantly
  • Price Comparison - See alternatives across price points
  • Save Favorites - Build your wishlist
  • Personalized Recommendations - Based on your style
